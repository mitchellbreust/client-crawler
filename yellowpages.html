Handy tips

Drip, drip, drip. Searching for a local plumber near you?

We've pulled together some questions for you to ask in your quest to find the right one.

When can you find the time to undertake the repair or address the fault?

Are you able to provide references from past plumbing projects?

Is your business covered by an insurance policy?

Are you a licensed plumber or gas fitter?

What are your qualifications?

How many years of industry experience to do you have under your belt?

Do you specialise in roof plumber jobs?

What is your pricing like and the associated service charges?

How are your charges calculated (time, or per task) and what payment methods do you take?

Where do you source your replacement parts from?

Is there a warranty offered on your work?

Asking these questions will help you feel confident that you have done adequate research. Then proceed to make a selection of the best plumber or gas fitter to undertake that repair, gas leak or faulty tap.
